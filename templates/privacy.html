Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information: your name and email address provided at registration.
  • Expense data: amounts, categories, dates, and notes you enter into the app.
  • Usage data: basic information about how you interact with the service, such as pages visited and actions taken.

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive financial credentials.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used solely to provide and improve Spendly. Specifically:

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To display and organise your expense history within the app.
  • To generate summaries and insights based on your spending patterns.
  • To respond to support requests or account-related queries.

We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell or rent your personal information to any third party.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure.

You may delete your account at any time. Upon deletion, your personal data and expense records will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly may use a limited number of trusted third-party services to operate — for example, hosting infrastructure or error monitoring tools. These providers are bound by confidentiality obligations and may only process your data as necessary to perform their service.

We do not integrate with any advertising networks, data brokers, or analytics platforms that track you across other websites.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Export your expense data at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us through the app's support channels.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the app's support channels. We aim to respond to all privacy-related enquiries within 7 business days.
